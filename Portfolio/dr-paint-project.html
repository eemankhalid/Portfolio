Dr. Paint

Dr. Paint is a web platform designed to showcase professional painting projects, services, and consultancy solutions. Built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for the frontend and MongoDB for the backend, the site offers a modern, user-friendly interface with a smooth experience.

Key Features:

  • Project Showcase: A gallery of past and ongoing painting projects, providing potential clients with a visual overview of the quality and variety of services.
  • Services Section: Detailed descriptions of various painting services, including residential, commercial, and industrial projects.
  • Consultancy: A dedicated section where users can get expert advice on color selection, surface preparation, and other painting-related queries.
  • Backend and Database: MongoDB ensures smooth data management for projects, customer inquiries, and services.

Technologies Used:

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Backend: MongoDB, Express Js, Node Js

User-Friendly Design:

The clean and simple layout ensures a smooth browsing experience. Clients can easily explore services or consult experts for personalized advice.
